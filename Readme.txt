The colorizr gem will format your output to nine different colors. It is easy to use and install.


--usage--
- Put require 'colorizr' at the top of your file.
- You can use String.sample_colors to see a list of colors
  available and see what they look like.
- You can also put "your string here".chosen-color-here
  will make your string that color.

--installation--
Once downloaded to your PC in terminal, navigate to the directory that colorizr
is in and then type without quotes "gem install ./colorizr-0.0.2.gem"
